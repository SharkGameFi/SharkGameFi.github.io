Our Game

SharkTown

A narrative-driven bar management sim set in a magical town where every cocktail you craft shapes the story. Think Stardew Valley meets mixology — forage for enchanted ingredients by day, brew magical drinks by night, and watch your choices ripple through the lives of a cast of deeply flawed, wonderfully complicated NPCs.

Business and relationships are mechanically interdependent — ignore your NPCs and they tank your bar
Multi-dimensional characters with branching storylines driven by your choices
Romance, rivalry, family secrets, and the occasional murder — all served over the counter
In Development · PC / Steam
